About Me

GeoAI LiDAR SLAM Sensor Fusion 3x Kaggle Expert

I am a Research Scholar in Geoinformatics at IIT Kanpur, working under the guidance of Prof. Salil Goel. My academic journey has taken me from Computer Science at MIT (Anna University) through Mathematics at IIT Madras, and now into the frontier of spatial intelligence research.

My work sits at the convergence of Sensor Fusion, LiDAR, SLAM, and Robotics for autonomous navigation. I approach these challenges with the mathematical rigor of a pure mathematician, the algorithmic intuition of a software engineer, and the data-driven mindset of a machine learning practitioner.

Beyond the lab, I am a 3x Kaggle Expert, a musician (keyboardist), currently learning German, and a polyglot who speaks six languages — Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Hindi, English, and Malayalam.
